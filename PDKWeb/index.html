Sri Satok Community Rehabilitation Organization (PDK) is a training center for individuals with disabilities (special needs). Established in July 2015 and fully operational starting September 2015, PDK Satok is fully funded by the Petra Jaya Welfare Association (PKPJ) before being inaugurated as a body under the Social Welfare Department on 13 April 2016. PDK Sri Satok has been officially registered as a Societies with the Registrar of Societies (ROS) in 2018.

PDK SRI SATOK PROFILING SYSTEM

The main function of the profiling system is to store trainee information who joined the PDK Sri Satok volunteering programs. In addition, the system for staff can view their profile, trainee profile and also generate report. 

Vision

Empowering individuals with disabilities (special needs) and presenting them as capable members of society in various fields.

Mission

To be a center that offers the best services and facilities to the community with disabilities (special needs).
